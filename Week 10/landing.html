Frequently Asked

Questions we often get

Do I need prior coding experience? ▼

No prior experience is required. The bootcamp starts from the very basics and progressively builds your skills. We do recommend completing our free 5-hour pre-work module before the cohort begins.

How many hours per week should I commit? ▼

Full-time cohort students should expect 40–50 hours per week. Part-time evening students typically commit 20–25 hours. Consistent daily practice is more important than marathon sessions.

What does the job guarantee actually cover? ▼

If you complete all projects, attend all sessions, and don't receive a job offer within 6 months of graduation, we refund your tuition in full. Terms and conditions apply — speak to an advisor for details.

Are the classes live or recorded? ▼

All sessions are live and interactive, with recordings available for 90 days afterward. Live attendance is strongly encouraged as real-time Q&A is a key part of the learning experience.

When does the next cohort start? ▼

The next cohort begins on 15 July 2025. Applications close on 30 June or when seats fill up — we cap cohorts at 30 students to maintain quality. Apply early to secure your spot.
